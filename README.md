## Swiggy Instamart Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

### Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **deep-dive analysis on Swiggy Instamart retail sales data** and building an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to identify key business insights.

### Dashboard

The dashboard helps analyze:

* Sales performance
* Product category contribution
* Outlet performance
* Customer rating trends
* Inventory distribution

The goal is to provide **data-driven insights that can help improve revenue, product strategy, and outlet performance.**

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI            | Value      |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| Total Sales    | **$1.20M** |
| Average Sales  | **$141**   |
| Total Items    | **8523**   |
| Average Rating | **3.9**    |

### KPI Insights

**1. Total Sales – $1.20M**

Indicates strong marketplace demand across multiple outlets.

**2. Average Sales – $141**

Suggests moderate order value which indicates **frequent smaller basket purchases** typical for instant delivery platforms.

**3. Total Items – 8523**

Large inventory size enabling **wide product availability and customer choice.**

**4. Average Rating – 3.9**

Customer satisfaction is relatively good but **there is room for improvement to reach 4.2+ ratings.**

---

# Deep Dive Analysis

## 1. Outlet Establishment Trend

The outlet establishment trend shows how sales evolve based on the year of outlet setup.

### Key Insights

* Sales increased significantly after **2017–2018 expansion**
* **Peak sales occurred in 2018 ($205K)** indicating strong growth phase
* Slight decline occurred after 2018 indicating **market saturation or competition**

### Business Insight

Swiggy should focus on **new city expansion strategies similar to 2018 growth phase.**

---

# 2. Fat Content Analysis

Sales split based on **Low Fat vs Regular products**

| Category | Sales |
| -------- | ----- |
| Regular  | $776K |
| Low Fat  | $425K |

### Insights

* **Regular products dominate the market**
* Low-fat products still contribute significantly but are less preferred

### Recommendation

Swiggy can introduce **health-focused marketing campaigns** to increase low-fat product sales.

# 4. Outlet Size Analysis

Sales distribution across outlet sizes.

| Outlet Size | Sales |
| ----------- | ----- |
| Medium      | $507K |
| Small       | $444K |
| High        | $248K |

### Insights

Medium outlets generate the **highest revenue**.

Large outlets do not necessarily lead to higher sales.

### Recommendation

Swiggy should prioritize **medium-sized fulfillment centers for efficiency and cost balance.**

# Key Business Insights

### Insight 1

Tier 3 cities generate the **highest revenue contribution**, indicating strong market penetration in smaller cities.

### Insight 2

Essential grocery categories such as **fruits, snacks, and dairy drive the majority of sales.**

### Insight 3

Medium sized outlets perform better than large outlets due to **operational efficiency.**

### Insight 4

Supermarket Type1 outlets dominate the platform’s sales.

### Insight 5

Customer ratings average **3.9**, meaning improving service quality could significantly improve retention.

---

# Recommendations

### 1. Expand in Tier 2 and Tier 3 Cities

Demand is highest in smaller cities.

### 2. Focus on High Demand Categories

Maintain strong inventory in:

* Fruits
* Snacks
* Dairy
* Frozen foods

### 3. Improve Customer Ratings

Introduce:

* Faster delivery
* Better packaging
* Quality control

### 4. Optimize Outlet Strategy

Medium-sized outlets provide the **best balance between cost and revenue.**
